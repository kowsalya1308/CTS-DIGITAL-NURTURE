Display Utilities for Sidebar

Utility Purpose
d-none Hide sidebar on all screen sizes
d-md-block Show as block on medium+ screens
d-none d-md-block Combined: Hide on mobile, show on tablet+
d-md-none Hide on medium+ screens (show on mobile)

Best Practices

  • Use d-none d-md-block to hide sidebars on mobile
  • Provide alternative navigation (hamburger menu) for mobile
  • Test on different screen sizes
  • Ensure important content isn't hidden on mobile
  • Consider touch-friendly interactions for mobile
