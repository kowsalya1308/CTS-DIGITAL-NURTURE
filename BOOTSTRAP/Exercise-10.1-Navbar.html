Exercise 10.1: Responsive Navbar

A responsive navbar with logo, navigation links, and search form.

Navbar Components Explained

navbar-brand

The logo or site name. Appears on the left.

navbar-toggler

Hamburger menu button that appears on mobile.

navbar-nav

Navigation links container.

dropdown

Dropdown menus for additional navigation.
